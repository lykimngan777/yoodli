The Challenge

Harness needed a way to ensure their technical sales teams could explain complex CI/CD concepts simply and effectively. Manual review of certification videos was taking weeks for the enablement team to process.

"We needed speed without sacrificing quality. Yoodli's AI grading allowed us to certify our entire global sales org in days instead of months."

The Solution

Harness deployed Yoodli for their quarterly product launch certification. Reps recorded their 5-minute pitches on Yoodli, which automatically flagged missing value propositions and technical inaccuracies.

The Result

  • 75% reduction in manual review time.
  • 100% of the sales team certified within 10 days.
  • Improved technical accuracy in customer discovery calls.
