Frequently asked questions about Yoodli for partner enablement

How does Yoodli support partner enablement programs?
Yoodli provides an automated platform for partners to practice discovery calls, product pitches, and objection handling. Managers can then certify partners based on their performance data rather than just completion of a video course.
Can Yoodli support partner certification?
Yes. You can set specific performance thresholds (rubrics) that partners must meet in AI roleplays to achieve certification, ensuring a consistent standard of quality across your ecosystem.
How does it ensure brand consistency across partners?
By providing a shared set of practice scenarios and AI feedback rubrics, you define exactly what "good" looks like, and the AI reinforces those standards with every partner interaction.
Is Yoodli suitable for global partner ecosystems?
Absolutely. Yoodli's cloud-based platform and automated coaching make it easy to scale training and certification across thousands of partners in different regions and time zones.
How do partner leaders measure readiness?
Leaders get access to a dashboard showing practice frequency, certification status, and granular performance metrics across their entire partner network.
