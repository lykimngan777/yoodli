Frequently asked questions about AI sales training

What is AI sales training?
AI sales training uses artificial intelligence to simulate real sales conversations, providing a safe and scalable environment for reps to practice, receive feedback, and improve their skills without needing a human coach for every session.
What is the ROI of AI sales training?
Companies using Yoodli report faster ramp times for new hires, higher quota attainment, and thousands of hours saved in manager coaching time.
Are AI roleplays effective for sales training?
Yes, research shows that active practice is significantly more effective than passive learning. Yoodli's AI provides realistic, high-fidelity simulations that mirror actual client interactions.
Does AI sales training replace human managers?
No, it empowers them. By handling the routine practice and basic feedback, AI allows managers to focus their high-value coaching time on strategic deal reviews and nuanced skill development.
How do we measure ROI from AI sales training?
Yoodli provides detailed analytics on skill improvement, practice frequency, and certification completion, which can be correlated with sales performance data in your CRM.
