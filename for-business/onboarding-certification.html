What is AI-powered onboarding and certification?

AI-powered onboarding and certification ensure employees prove they can execute, not just complete training.

With Yoodli, new hires and existing team members use AI roleplays to rehearse real-world scenarios aligned to your organization's standards. Each session is evaluated using defined rubrics and methodology, providing objective validation of readiness.

The result is faster ramp time and more consistent certification outcomes across teams and regions.

How to use AI for onboarding and certification

Organizations use AI in onboarding and certification in three primary ways:

Structured, roleplay-based onboarding

New hires practice realistic scenarios from day one. Instead of waiting for live situations, employees build confidence through simulations aligned to their role.

Objective certification

Team members demonstrate skill through evaluated roleplays tied to clear standards. Certification is based on performance, not attendance.

Reinforcement beyond certification

Skills are reinforced after onboarding to maintain consistency as messaging, products, and expectations evolve.

This creates a repeatable system for ramp and readiness, not a one-time milestone.
