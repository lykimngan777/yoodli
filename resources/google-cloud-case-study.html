How Google Cloud used Yoodli AI to rollout new GTM pitch certification to 15,000+ employees (with 92% CSAT)

October 24, 2023 • 7 min read

The Problem

The Google Cloud Go-to-Market (GTM) team needed to roll out a new strategy across their org. This involved certifying over 15,000 employees on their pitch within 3 months. Typically, such a certification would involve one-on-one roleplaying, manager reviews, and multiple rounds of feedback – a process that is usually extremely painful.

"Yoodli made roleplay practice fun, scalable and easy to implement. It would have been impossible to ensure that all of our employees used the same pitch without using an AI strategy like Yoodli."
— Amit Vidwans, Strategy at Google Cloud (Yoodli Implementation Lead)

The Solution

Google Cloud used Yoodli's AI voice coach to create a bespoke pitch certification program. All customers around the Google Cloud method through Yoodli, the Cloud enablement and L&D team was able to:

  • Create Google Cloud specific scenarios and objections for learners to practice
  • Build custom rubrics that the AI would grade learners against
  • Customize talking points that learners should hit
  • View aggregate progress on their admin dashboard

The Outcome

The results were staggering. The training had a CSAT of 92% (compared to 80% target). The training program completion rate was 30% higher than previous Google Cloud trainings.
