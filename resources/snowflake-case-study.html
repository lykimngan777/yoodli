How Snowflake Saved 1,200+ Hours of Manager Coaching per Quarter with Yoodli AI

November 12, 2023 • 6 min read

The Challenge

Snowflake's rapid growth meant hiring hundreds of new sales representatives every month. Managers were overwhelmed with "pitch checks" and basic communication coaching, leaving little time for high-level strategy and complex deal closing.

"Yoodli allowed our managers to focus on the last mile of coaching while the AI handled the fundamental repetition and confidence building."
— Head of Sales Enablement, Snowflake

The Solution

By integrating Yoodli into their onboarding workflow, new hires practiced their initial pitches at least 20 times before ever presenting to a human manager. Yoodli provided instant feedback on pacing, clarity, and keyword inclusion.

The Result

  • 1,200+ hours saved per quarter for Sales Managers.
  • 25% faster ramp-up time for new Account Executives.
  • Consistent messaging across global sales territories.
