Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have instructional videos where I can get started?

Yes! We have a library of tutorials and onboarding videos in our Help Center. You can also find tips and tricks on our YouTube channel to help you make the most of Yoodli's AI coaching.

How secure is my data?

Data security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure your data is safe. We never sell your personal information or training data to third parties.

What about pricing?

Yoodli offers a free tier for individuals to practice and get basic feedback. For organizations and power users, we offer premium plans with advanced analytics, team management, and custom persona configurations. Check our Pricing page for details.

I’m looking for a coach/want to meet other users. Where should I go?

We have a vibrant community of speakers and coaches. You can join our LinkedIn group or participate in our regular webinars to connect with other Yoodli users and experts.

Can I use Yoodli on my everyday calls?

Yes! Yoodli can be integrated with platforms like Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams to provide real-time analysis during your actual meetings.

I’m a communication coach. How can I use Yoodli?

Communication coaches use Yoodli to provide objective data to their clients. You can set up custom curriculum, track client progress, and use Yoodli's analytics to supplement your personalized coaching.

Can I use Yoodli in multiple languages?

While our primary focus is English, we are constantly expanding our language capabilities. Stay tuned for updates on multi-language support!

Vẫn còn câu hỏi?

Nếu bạn không tìm thấy câu trả lời mình cần, đừng ngần ngại liên hệ với đội ngũ hỗ trợ của chúng tôi.
